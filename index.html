About

I am an XR developer and software engineer from Accra, Ghana, turning bold ideas into immersive realities for over 7 years. As one of the early pioneers of XR in Ghana and Africa since 2016, I’ve been obsessed with pushing what’s possible in virtual, augmented, and mixed reality. I started out building games in Unity and C#, then quickly moved into crafting XR experiences for education, training, and real-time collaboration where impact meets innovation. As Co-Founder and CTO of Relu Interactives, I lead teams building next-gen spatial computing solutions and multi-user XR platforms that help people learn, work, and connect in entirely new ways. My toolkit spans Unity3D (7+ years), XR SDKs (Meta Quest, Apple Vision Pro), WebXR, and full-stack development (React, Node.js, Python) powered by modern cloud infrastructure. I’m especially excited about the intersection of XR, education, and collaboration designing systems that don’t just look futuristic, but actually solve real-world problems. I regularly share insights through technical writing, workshops, and research-driven collaborations, always exploring the next frontier in immersive computing.
