About

I am a passionate and self taught software engineer from Accra, Ghana. I love Web development, game development and XR(AR, VR, MR). Growing up as a child i always had love for tech. This pushed me into learning c++, c#, html,css,js and game development with unity. My love for games was so overwhelming that i enjoyed developing my own games and sharing with friends. Over the years i have been intrigued by how technology is evolving. I spend most of my days working as Fullstack Software Engineer. I primarily work on building new software features, software architecture planning, sprints planning, developing api's and integrate third party api's and payment services into software applications. During my weekends and free time i like to spend time researching into XR and building games. I love to learn and i don't have a favorite language or framework, i adjust when the need arises and learn what needs to get the job done.
